Our Story

It all started with Holly, a lovable and huggable chocolate lab who captured the hearts of everyone around her. Her puppy parents, passionate about providing the best for their furry friend, struggled to find high-quality treats that were both safe, good looking, and enjoyable for Holly.

Determined to give Holly and other pups like her the best possible care, they set out to create The Barkery. Our mission is to provide products for the well-being of “the whole pet” inside and out. The Barkery offers top-notch baked goods that will keep your furry friend healthy and happy.

At our bakery, we use only the best human-grade ingredients to create delicious treats that will have your dog begging for more. We believe that treats shouldn't just be tasty - they should also be good for your dog's health. That's why we use wholesome ingredients like whole wheat flour, fresh fruits, and vegetables to create our treats.

At The Barkery, we understand that your dog is more than just a pet - they're a beloved member of your family. That's why we treat every dog that comes through our doors with the same love and care that we would want for our own Holly.

The Barkery. A bakery for dogs.
